It depends on the individual. In Queensland, learner drivers must complete a minimum of 100 logged hours before sitting their practical test. We cover South Brisbane and surrounding suburbs including West End, Woolloongabba, Annerley, Yeronga, Moorooka, Salisbury, Rocklea, Tarragindi, Greenslopes, Holland Park, Mount Gravatt, Sunnybank, Runcorn, Coopers Plains and more. Not sure if we cover your area? Just get in touch and we'll let you know.
All lessons are in an automatic vehicle. This means you'll be eligible for an automatic licence on passing your test. If you specifically need manual, get in touch and we can discuss options.
Bring your current Queensland learner licence — you must have it on you to legally drive on public roads. Wear comfortable, flat shoes. Your logbook is also useful so we can sign off your hours after each session.
Payment is taken at the end of each lesson. We accept cash and card. Lesson packages are paid upfront. We'll confirm all pricing before your first lesson — no surprises.
